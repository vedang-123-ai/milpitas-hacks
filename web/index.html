How to play
Two ways to "touch" a pad: click the dots on screen, OR press the keys (P1 = 1-6, P2 = Q W E R T Y). A letter = a set of dots.
Find-target: it says "Touch the letter A" → click dot 1 (or press 1). B=1+2 · C=1+4 · E=1+5. Wrong dot = gentle buzz, no penalty.
Commands: click the buttons above (1 Player, 2 Player, Easy/Medium/Hard), or say them into the mic.
Dashed ring = a dot in the current target · green = touched.
