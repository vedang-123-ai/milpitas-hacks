how to play ›
Two ways to “touch” a pad: click the dots on screen, or press the keys (P1 = 1–6, P2 = Q W E R T Y). A letter = a set of dots.
Find-target: it says “Touch the letter A” → click dot 1 (or press 1). B = 1+2 · C = 1+4 · E = 1+5. A wrong dot is a gentle buzz, never a penalty.
Commands: use the buttons above, or say them into the mic. A dashed ring marks a needed dot; a lit dot is held.
